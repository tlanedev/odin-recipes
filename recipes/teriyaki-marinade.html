Teriyaki Marinade

Description

Teriyaki sauce is a wonderful balance of ginger, garlic and soy. Use this marinade to enhance your meat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 2 garlice cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp ground ginger
  • 4 tbsp brown sugar
  • 4 tbsp finely chopped white onion
  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 1/2 tsp pepper

Steps

  1. Mix all of the ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. To marinate your meat, place your meat in a zip lock bag with the marinade. Place in fridge overnight.
