Teriyaki Stir Fry

Description

Stir fry is a fun dish. It usually has bold flavors, meat or fried tofu, and crunchy veggies. This recipe is no exception. Odin recipes recommends using this teriyaki marinade and serving your stir fry atop cooked rice

Ingredients

  • a protein: beef, chicken, pork or tofu cut into bite-size chunks
  • enough teriyaki marinade to coat the
  • broccoli florets
  • julienned carrots
  • julienned red pepper
  • cooked rice
  • canola oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the protein by marinading it over night.
  2. Heat a wok to high heat and oil it with a small amount of canola oil.
  3. Cook the meat in the wok until it is halfway done. Stir constantly.
  4. Add the veggies (broccoli, carrots, and red pepper) and continue stirring constantly. Cook until both the meat and veggies are done.
  5. Remove the stir fry from the wok and serve atop the cooked rice. Enjoy!
